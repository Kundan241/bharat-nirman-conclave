About the Conclave

A National Summit for Digital India

The Bharat Nirman Conclave is a premier gathering of entrepreneurs, policymakers, and digital business leaders committed to building a self-reliant India through technology and infrastructure.

This conclave brings together changemakers to explore how Domestic Capability Centres, regulatory compliance, and digital enablement can power the next phase of India's economic growth.

Hosted by Bharat Office Setu (BOS Services), this is a landmark event shaping the future of Indian entrepreneurship on a national scale.
